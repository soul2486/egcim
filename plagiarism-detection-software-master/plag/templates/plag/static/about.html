About Us

In 2012 we had a simple goal: Help our customers protect their content by effectively detecting plagiarism. To meet this goal we developed Plagiarism Guard, an online system which automatically scans content and flags up suspected cases of plagiarism.

Our founder Tristan Perry is a software developer with a passion for plagiarism detection, natural language processing and computational linguistics.

We are continuously making improvements to our software and we are one of the most flexible plagiarism detection companies, supporting a range of content types and allowing you to choose how frequently your content is scanned.

Plagiarism Guard is based in the United Kingdom and we have customers from around the World. Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions, comments or suggestions.
